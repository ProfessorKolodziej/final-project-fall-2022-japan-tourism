Enjoy Kobe

  1. Kobe Harborland: This waterfront district is a hub of entertainment, shopping, and dining. Take a leisurely stroll along the harbor, explore the boutiques, and enjoy the stunning night views.
  2. Mount Rokko: Offering panoramic views of the city, Mount Rokko is a great place for hiking, especially during the autumn season when the foliage is breathtaking.
  3. Kobe Nunobiki Herb Gardens: These beautiful gardens feature a variety of herbs, flowers, and greenery. You can explore walking trails and enjoy a cable car ride with scenic vistas.
  4. Kobe Chinatown (Nankinmachi): This vibrant and bustling Chinatown is filled with delicious street food and restaurants serving Chinese cuisine. It's a must-visit for food enthusiasts.
  5. Kobe Port Tower: An iconic symbol of Kobe, the Port Tower offers an observation deck with spectacular views of the city and port area.
  6. Arima Onsen: Just a short train ride away from Kobe, Arima Onsen is one of Japan's oldest and most famous hot spring resorts. Relax in its soothing waters and explore the charming historic streets.
  7. Kobe Animal Kingdom: If you're traveling with family, this zoo offers an interactive experience where you can get up close with various animals, making it a fun and educational destination.
  8. Kobe Earthquake Memorial Museum: Learn about the devastating Great Hanshin Earthquake of 1995 and its impact on Kobe through interactive exhibits and personal stories.
